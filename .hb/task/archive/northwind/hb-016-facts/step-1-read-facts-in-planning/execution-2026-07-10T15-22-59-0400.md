`hb-sdk facts read` immediately before reading their respective tickets, and

generation and gap analysis (`grep -c facts` on both files was `0`); after, it
surfaces automatically without needing to be restated in `ticket.md`. Scope
held additive-only: no existing step's content or step-numbering semantics
changed except being shifted down by one; no Python, tests, or other skill
files touched.
