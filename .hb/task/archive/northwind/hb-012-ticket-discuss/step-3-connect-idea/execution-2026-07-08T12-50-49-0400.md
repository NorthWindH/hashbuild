## Deviations from plan

**One necessary correction the plan did not anticipate**: renumbering the old Step 6 (Emit
ticket) to Step 7 orphaned two internal step references. The pre-edit Step 3 said "skip to
Step 6" (no-Jira-MCP-found branch) and "'no' → ... go to Step 6" (decline-to-push branch); the
pre-edit Step 4's abort bullet said "skip to Step 6." All three pointed at the *old* Step 6
(Emit ticket, the correct destination for those branches — none of which produce a pushed
Epic). Left as literal "Step 6," they would now incorrectly point at the *new* Step 6 (Offer
Jira Idea link), which requires `$JIRA == "pushed"` and would never actually run for those
callers — but the misdirected label would still mislead a reader following the flow.

**Fix applied**: updated all three references to say "Step 7," preserving their original
semantic destination (Emit ticket) under its new number. This means Step 3's and Step 4's
*text* is no longer byte-identical to the pre-edit file, contradicting the plan's §0.2 claim
that "Detect MCP + collect NL | Step 3 | untouched." However, the plan's own intent — no
functional change to Steps 1–4's branching logic — is fully preserved; the corrected
destination step content (Emit ticket) is itself byte-identical to the pre-edit Step 6, just
under a new number. The plan's structural check "Steps 1–3 text byte-unchanged" (§5) is
technically not met for Step 3, but its unstated *purpose* (no behavior drift) is met, and
leaving the dangling numeric reference wrong would have been a functional bug: a future reader
or maintainer editing Step 6/7 boundaries would be misled. No other deviations.
