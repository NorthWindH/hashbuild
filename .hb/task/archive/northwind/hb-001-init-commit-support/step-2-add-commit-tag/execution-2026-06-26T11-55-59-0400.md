## Outcome

Before this step, `commit write-message-file task` and `task-step` produced subjects like `hb-001: short` and `hb-001/step-2: short` with no indication of which lifecycle skill produced the commit. After this step, callers can pass `--tag <slug>` to inject `(<tag>)` immediately after the colon-space separator: `hb-001: (task-create) short` and `hb-001/step-2: (step-execute) short`. The change is additive only — all callers that omit `--tag` produce byte-for-byte identical output to before. All 8 in-scope skill files now pass their designated tags, and `committing.md` documents the new flag. 107/107 tests green; scope held to exactly 11 files.
